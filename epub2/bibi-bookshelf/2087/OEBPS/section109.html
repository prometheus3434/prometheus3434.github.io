I have a question for you: ահա՛ւասիկ

 

where does it come from? is it used only by bolsohay?

 

CEVAP

 

The great dictionary of the Mkhitareans (1836 ed.) has the triple declension of ahavasig (behold this), ahavatig (behold that), ahavanig (behold it). I suppose the structure is quite clear, aha (exclamation) + asig’ (this one).

 

The dictionary only cites examples from Dônanamag, an undated collection of sermons, so I don’t know if the usage goes back to the classical era or not. Similarly, Acharian cites no classical examples.
