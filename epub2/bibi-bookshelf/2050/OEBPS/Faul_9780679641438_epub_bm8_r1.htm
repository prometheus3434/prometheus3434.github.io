VINTAGE INTERNATIONAL

William Faulkner, Toni Morrison, Doris Lessing, V. S. Naipaul, Eudora Welty, Joan Didion, and Cormac McCarthy, among many others: VINTAGE INTERNATIONAL is a bold series of books devoted to publishing the best writing of the last century from the world over. Offering both classic and contemporary fiction and literary nonfiction, VINTAGE INTERNATIONAL aims to introduce a new generation of readers to world-class writing that has stood the test of time and essential works by the preeminent authors of today.
