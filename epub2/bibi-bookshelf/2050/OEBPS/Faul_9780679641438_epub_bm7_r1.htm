ACADEMIC RESOURCES FOR EDUCATORS

Committed to publishing intellectually important works and enduring classics, Vintage Books and Anchor Books are the American publishers of William Faulkner, Albert Camus, Willa Cather, James Joyce, Thomas Mann, Ralph Ellison, Franz Kafka, and Toni Morrison, as well as Michel Foucault, Simone de Beauvoir, Edward Said, and James Baldwin, among many others.

Random House’s Academic Resources website—www.randomhouse.com/academic—presents over 5,000 academic titles in a searchable database by discipline. This site also provides information about requesting desk and examination copies; makes available teacher’s guides and recent subject catalogs for download or request by mail; posts our academic conference schedule; and offers the opportunity to sign up for our e-newsletters, which provide advance information about our publishing program.
